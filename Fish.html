Mahi Mahi

Mahi Mahi or "Dolphinfish" is an extremely streamline and colorful fish that has become famous for its delicious meat and commercial fishing value. The name mahi-mahi is from the Hwaiian language and means "very strong". Male mahi-mahi have a prominent froehead where as females have a far more rounded head and out of the water the fish had a golden shine. The fish can live upwards of five years and they typically travel in groups of 4.

Black Marlin

Black Marlins are found in tropical and subtropical areas in the Indian and Pacific Oceans. They are one of the largest species of marlin and are one of the fastest species of fish on the planet with a maximum speed of 65 mph. Black Marlin like mahi-mahi are considered to be a prized commercial fishing catch and weigh upwards of 150 lbs

Pocket Shark

The pocket shark is one of the rarest species of shark in the world and can only be found off of Chile in the southeastern Pacific Ocean. The pocket shark can be easily distinguished from other sharks due to the two pockets located near the frontal fins of the fish. The pocket shark is a reasonably small shark weighing upwards of 15 grams and has a very clyndrical and streamline body.
